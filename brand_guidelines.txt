TODDE BRAND COLORS
==================

Primary Colors:
- Deep Ocean Blue: #004080 (Primary brand color - headers, buttons, key elements)
- Tangerine Orange: #E18335 (Secondary/Accent - highlights, pricing, CTAs)
- Sky Blue: #89CFF0 (Light accent - backgrounds, hover states)

Supporting Colors:
- Dark Blue: #003366 (Hover states, darker elements)
- Dark Orange: #C66D2A (Text, secondary orange elements)
- Cream: #FFF5ED (Light backgrounds, cards)
- Neutral Gray: #F3F7F8 (Page background)
- Dark Gray: #383838 (Body text, secondary buttons)
- Muted Gray: #6E7A7B (Subtitles, muted text)

Color Usage Guidelines:
- Use Deep Ocean Blue (#004080) for primary brand elements, navigation, and main CTAs
- Use Tangerine Orange (#E18335) for accents, pricing, and attention-drawing elements
- Use Sky Blue (#89CFF0) for light accents and success states
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Avoid using all three primary colors equally - let Deep Ocean Blue dominate
